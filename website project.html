All About Tiger Species

Explore the different subspecies of tigers around the world.

Bengal Tiger

Scientific Name: Panthera tigris tigris

Found mainly in India. Known for its powerful build and majestic appearance.

Siberian Tiger

Scientific Name: Panthera tigris altaica

Largest tiger subspecies. Native to Russia's Far East and Northeast China.

Sumatran Tiger

Scientific Name: Panthera tigris sumatrae

Smallest of all tiger species. Found only on the island of Sumatra in Indonesia.

Malayan Tiger

Scientific Name: Panthera tigris jacksoni

Critically endangered. Lives in the tropical forests of the Malay Peninsula.
